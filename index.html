Labyrinth game




The game

The rooms of the labyrinth are represented by the cells of a 7x7 table. Every room has a given number of doors. If two adjacent rooms both have doors on their mutual wall, then the player shall enter from one room to the other. The even rows and columns of the table can be slided, the odd ones are fixed. By sliding the rooms, new routes open in the labyrinth so that the player can reach their treasures. The first player to find all their treasures and get back to their starting corner shall win.
